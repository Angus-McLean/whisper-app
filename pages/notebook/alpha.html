Design Evolution

From the feedback and usability testing results given by Group 3, we have decided to make the following design changes to improve the usability of our product:

  1. Clearer distinction between whisper call and whisper voice recordings

Both test subjects were confused about the distinction of the icon between ‘whisper call’ and ‘whisper voice recordings’. To improve recognition, we have changed the icons for a clearer distinction between them.

“The user was initially confused by the difference between the voice messages and whisper calls,”

“The symbols used to differentiate between the whisper call and voice message were not different and expressive enough.”

  1. Clearer distinction between whisper voice recordings for private and group whispers

This goes with point 5, where we will improve our system with a clearer description of the current status

  1. Include the full name of people seated

As suggested by Group 3, we will include the full name of the people seated at the seating plan step so that users can identify their friends in cases of similar initials.

“As suggested above, allowing students to put full names could help individuals to locate the specific group they wish to be in.”

  1. Flexible seating to cater group of different sizes

Keeping in mind the different use cases, we will adapt a flexible seating plan with varying numbers of seats in a group.

“We also noted that expanding the number of group members could make this application more flexible to varying friend group sizes.”

  1. Centralized ‘whisper call’ and ‘whisper recording’ buttons

Originally, in order to initiate private whispers, the user would need to hover over the person on the seating plan and click on either the ‘whisper call’ or the ‘whisper recording’ button. As suggested by Group 3 in the formative feedback, we have decided to keep only the 2 buttons at the bottom (next to ‘Type a message’), and delete the 2 that appear when hovering on the person. Instead, to initiate a private whisper, one would only need to hover and click on the person, and then proceed to click on either the ‘whisper call’ or the ‘whisper voice messages’ button at the bottom.

This improvement will decrease the amount of buttons that the users will have to learn (‘which is for private whispers?’ ‘which is for group whispers?’) and reduce confusion. It will also make it easier for users to switch between group whispers and private whispers because they will realize that private whispers can be accessed by clicking on the said person, while group whispers can be accessed by deselecting the said person.

Before: hover over a person to initiate private whisper with 2 buttons                After: hover over a person to start private whisper and use the same buttons at the bottom
